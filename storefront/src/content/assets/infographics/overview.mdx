Infographics are used to communicate large amounts of information or data and to tell complex stories. The challenge is to present stories in a way that is easy for people to digest and understand, in a consistent style that makes Equinor distinctive and easily recognisable.


**Brand Center toolbox**

Please refer to the Equinor [Brand Center](https://communicationtoolbox.equinor.com/brandcenter/en/equinorbc/component/default/104 "Brand Center Toolbox Guidelines") for guidelines on making infographics.

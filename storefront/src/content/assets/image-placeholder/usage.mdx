## Figma

### How to add
Select "Assets" (:warning: link to Getting Started > Figma, Finding components) and find the image placeholder elements by either scrolling to the section called "Image placeholder" or searching for "image placeholder". Expand the relevant section and drag the placeholder component in.


### How to use
To fill the placeholder with an image, locate the "Fill" section. Expand the "Image" details section, then hover the existimg image to click "Choose Image" to choose a new image. When resizing the image placeholder, hold the `Shift` key to keep the aspect ratio.

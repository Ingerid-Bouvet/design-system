## Figma

* Select the element you want to apply color to. 
* In the sidebar under 'Fill', pick the styles menu. This is the button with the 4 dots.
  * :warning: Image example like: https://i.imgur.com/yYEWW0x.png
* The styles menu will open and you can choose the colour you need.
  * :warning: Image example like: https://i.imgur.com/k1wgbNX.png
* Once you have chosen a colour, the 'Fill' section will change to show you the colour selected.
  * :warning: Image example like: https://i.imgur.com/VcbeFU9.png
